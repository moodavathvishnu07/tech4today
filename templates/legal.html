Legal & Fair Use

Codly Technical Intelligence ("Codly Hub") is an AI-powered news aggregation and analytical platform designed for researchers, builders, and technology enthusiasts.

1. Fair Use & Attribution

Codly Hub utilizes various RSS feeds and public data sources to provide real-time technical intelligence. All original content, headlines, and trademarks belong to their respective copyright holders. We provide clear attribution and direct links to the original sources for every pulse detected.

Our AI-generated summaries and sentiment analysis are "transformative works" intended to provide context and speed of discovery, falling under Fair Use (US) and Fair Dealing (India/Intl) frameworks.

2. Data Sources

We aggregate data from high-authority technical publishers. While we strive for absolute precision in our AI Pulse engine, we encourage users to verify all findings at the original source provided.

3. Non-Commercial Intent

Codly Hub serves as a technical intelligence showcase. We do not store full articles or override publisher paywalls. Our mission is to increase traffic to high-velocity technical journalism.

4. Takedown Requests

Publishers who wish to have their feeds removed from the Codly Global Hub or Local Core can contact us at legal@codly.io. Requests will be processed within 24 hours.
