Privacy Policy

Your privacy is paramount. This policy outlines how Codly Hub handles data to provide a personalized technical intelligence experience.

1. Data Collection

We collect minimal data necessary for core features:

  • Location Data: We use IP-based location (ipapi) to determine if you should see the Indian Local Core or Global Hub by default.
  • Preference Data: We store your theme (Light/Dark) and Vibe (On/Off) settings in your local browser storage.

2. Cookies

Codly Hub uses essential cookies to track your regional preference (`codly_country`) and view mode (`codly_view_mode`). These are used solely for content localization and do not track you across other websites.

3. Local Storage

We use browser Local Storage to cache last sync times and theme settings locally on your device to minimize server load and improve performance.

4. Third-Party Services

We use DucksDuckGo and Google favicon services to display publisher icons. These services may collect basic request metadata as per their own privacy policies.

5. Play Store Compliance

This application does not collect personally identifiable information (PII), names, emails, or phone numbers. All news processing happens on our secure backend without linking data to individual users.
